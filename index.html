WebGL Wireframe Demo (2019)

This demo is an embedded Unity-WebGL application, it shows a 3D-wireframe model of the Hochschule fuer Kuenste Bremen. It was made to preview different 3D-models of different kind with different art styles, such as wireframe optic or coloured point clouds.
You can rotate around the model and zoom.
