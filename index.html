WebGL Wireframe Demo (2019)

This demo is an embedded Unity-WebGL application, it shows a 3D-wireframe model of the Hochschule fuer Kuenste Bremen. It was made to preview different 3D-models of different kind with different art styles, such as wireframe optic or coloured point clouds.
You can rotate around the model and zoom.

Go to the Wireframe Demo.

WebGL Lighting Demo (January 2020)

The Unity WebGL Lighting Demo shows the usage and performance of Unity WebGL applications with it's embedded Universal Render Pipeline. It features assets made by my friend and colleague 'uncle finnB0'.
Use 'WSAD'-keys while holding the right mouse button (it's the right one) and draggin the mouse to fly through the scene.
