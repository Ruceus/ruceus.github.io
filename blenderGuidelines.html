Blender Guidelines

Ever had a problem with blender? #meToo
So, here's a list of problems things that helped me working with blender, all this for the fresh version 2.8!

Weird behaviour with paste flipped keyframes

When I wanted to flip the animations for my run-cycle they were flipped totally wrong and not on x-axis. This solved my issue:
BTW!: Always make things in blender with teh front facing the Y-axis, left and right the X-axis and Z to the top!

On blenderartists I found this solution by user Sago:

  • delete all keyframes of the animation, and select all bones in Pose Mode and clear rotation (Alt+R) and position (Alt+G)
  • in Object mode, go to Top view (Numpad 7) and Alt+R the Armature, then rotate it 90 degrees on Z-axis (it should face front view now)
  • to apply rotation, press Ctrl+N Ctrl+A and choose Rotation
    •

    - Sago, November 2010

    It worked fine for me, even though I had to re-do all my animations. Always check your models, riggs and armature for correct positions and rotations! And I didn't even had to re-rotate by 90 degrees.
